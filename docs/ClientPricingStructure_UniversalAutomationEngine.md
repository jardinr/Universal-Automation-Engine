# Client Pricing Structure: Universal Automation Engine

This document outlines the business model and pricing structure for the **Universal Automation Engine**, a modular and interchangeable platform designed to solve unique automation challenges for various companies. Our goal is to provide flexible, value-driven solutions that empower businesses to streamline operations, enhance efficiency, and achieve significant return on investment.

## Our Value Proposition

The Universal Automation Engine transforms complex, manual processes into efficient, AI-driven workflows. By leveraging a modular architecture, we can rapidly deploy tailored solutions for data extraction, AI-powered categorization, and seamless integration with existing business platforms (CRMs, project management tools, etc.). This leads to:

*   **Increased Efficiency:** Automate repetitive tasks, freeing up valuable human resources.
*   **Enhanced Accuracy:** Reduce human error through standardized, AI-driven processing.
*   **Faster Response Times:** Accelerate client communication and internal processes.
*   **Scalability:** Solutions designed to grow with your business needs.
*   **Customization:** Adaptable to unique challenges across diverse industries.

## Business Model: Hybrid Approach

Our business model combines a **one-time setup and customization fee** with a **recurring monthly subscription** for ongoing service, maintenance, and support. This ensures a clear initial investment for tailored development and a predictable operational cost for continuous value.

## Pricing Structure: Tiered for Flexibility

We offer tiered pricing to accommodate businesses of varying sizes and complexity requirements. Each tier is designed to provide increasing levels of automation capacity, support, and advanced features.

### Tier 1: Basic Automation (Ideal for Small Businesses & Startups)

*   **Target:** Businesses with straightforward automation needs, lower transaction volumes, and a single primary workflow.
*   **Features:**
    *   **1 Core Workflow:** (e.g., Email Triage to CRM)
    *   **1 Data Source Integration:** (e.g., Email Inbox)
    *   **1 AI Model Integration:** (e.g., Basic Categorization)
    *   **1 Action Platform Integration:** (e.g., HubSpot Starter, Trello)
    *   **Standard Support:** Email support during business hours.
    *   **Monthly Usage Limit:** Up to 1,000 automated tasks/month.
*   **Pricing:**
    *   **One-Time Setup & Customization Fee:** $500 - $1,500 (depending on complexity)
    *   **Monthly Subscription:** $199 - $349

### Tier 2: Advanced Automation (Ideal for Growing Businesses)

*   **Target:** Businesses requiring multiple, more complex workflows, higher transaction volumes, and several integrations.
*   **Features:**
    *   **Up to 3 Core Workflows:** (e.g., Email Triage, Lead Qualification, Customer Support Routing)
    *   **Up to 3 Data Source Integrations:** (e.g., Email, Webhook, CSV Upload)
    *   **Up to 2 AI Model Integrations:** (e.g., Categorization, Sentiment Analysis)
    *   **Up to 2 Action Platform Integrations:** (e.g., HubSpot Professional, Salesforce, Asana)
    *   **Priority Support:** Faster response times, dedicated account manager.
    *   **Monthly Usage Limit:** Up to 5,000 automated tasks/month.
*   **Pricing:**
    *   **One-Time Setup & Customization Fee:** $1,500 - $4,000 (depending on complexity)
    *   **Monthly Subscription:** $499 - $899

### Tier 3: Enterprise Automation (Ideal for Large Organizations & High Volume)

*   **Target:** Large enterprises with extensive and highly customized automation needs, very high transaction volumes, and deep integration requirements.
*   **Features:**
    *   **Unlimited Core Workflows:** Fully customized to business operations.
    *   **Unlimited Data Source Integrations:** Any required source.
    *   **Multiple AI Model Integrations:** Advanced AI, custom ML models.
    *   **Multiple Action Platform Integrations:** Full suite of business tools.
    *   **24/7 Dedicated Support:** On-call support, strategic consulting.
    *   **High Volume Usage:** Custom task limits based on needs.
*   **Pricing:**
    *   **One-Time Setup & Customization Fee:** Custom Quote (starting from $5,000)
    *   **Monthly Subscription:** Custom Quote (starting from $1,000)

## Additional Services (Optional)

*   **Backlog Clearing Project:** For existing data backlogs (e.g., 1000+ emails), a separate project fee will be quoted based on volume and complexity.
*   **Custom Adapter Development:** Development of new data source, AI model, or action platform adapters not covered in standard tiers.
*   **Advanced Analytics & Reporting:** Custom dashboards and insights into automation performance.
*   **On-site Training & Workshops:** Tailored training for client teams.
